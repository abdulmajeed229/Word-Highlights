Word Highlights






Saylani Welfare Trust is one of Pakistan's most prominent and respected charitable organizations, dedicated to alleviating the suffering of the poor and underprivileged. Founded in 1999 by Maulana Bashir Farooq Qadri, the trust has grown into a multifaceted welfare organization that addresses various aspects of social welfare, including healthcare, education, hunger relief, and disaster management. At the heart of Saylani Welfare Trust's mission is the commitment to feeding the hungry. The organization runs one of the largest food distribution programs in Pakistan, serving free meals to thousands of people daily. These efforts have become a lifeline for many, particularly in urban areas where poverty and hunger are rampant. The trust ensures that no one goes to bed hungry, a mission that resonates deeply with its donors and volunteers. In addition to hunger relief, Saylani Welfare Trust is heavily involved in healthcare services. The trust operates hospitals, clinics, and mobile medical units that provide free or low-cost medical care to those in need. Their healthcare initiatives include general medicine, specialized treatments, surgeries, and even rehabilitation services. These services are especially vital in a country where access to quality healthcare is often limited by financial constraints. Education is another cornerstone of Saylani Welfare Trust's work. The organization runs several schools, vocational training centers, and scholarship programs aimed at empowering the youth with knowledge and skills. By providing education to underprivileged children and adults, the trust helps break the cycle of poverty, offering a path to a better future. Beyond these core areas, Saylani Welfare Trust is also known for its swift response to natural disasters and emergencies. Whether it's providing relief to flood victims, offering shelter to the homeless, or distributing essentials during the COVID-19 pandemic, the trust is always at the forefront of humanitarian efforts. Saylani Welfare Trust's work is fueled by the generosity of donors and the dedication of volunteers who share a common goal: to make the world a better place for those less fortunate. Through its wide-ranging initiatives, the trust has become a symbol of hope, compassion, and selfless service in Pakistan and beyond. In summary, Saylani Welfare Trust is more than just a charitable organization; it is a movement dedicated to uplifting humanity. Its comprehensive approach to social welfare continues to impact countless lives, making it a true beacon of hope for the needy.
